Company

Profile

Founded in 1968, Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group Company currently employs 32,000 staff with total assets of 34.8 billion CNY. It ranks 281st in the 2022 Fortune China 500 list and 129th in the Top 500 Chinese Manufacturers list. As a significant producer of military vehicles in China, it encompasses complete vehicle manufacturing, components research and development, production, sales, and after-sales services.

 

SAG Commercial Vehicle Company, a core subsidiary of Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group Company, locates in Baoji City, Shaanxi Province. The factory covers an area of 2,000 acres and has a total investment of 4.576 billion CNY. In accord with the Industry 3.0 standards, it has a comprehensive production capacity of 200,000 commercial vehicles per year. The product portfolio spans over 200 categories, including light, medium, and heavy-duty trucks, new energy vehicles, and  special vehicles.

 

Over the past decade, we have been dedicating to expanding our international business. It has successfully exported over 230,000 complete vehicles to more than 140 countries and regions around the world. We have achieved certifications such as the Technical Regulations of the Customs Union conformity Certificates (CU-TR), the Economic Commission of Europe certification (ECE) for the Asia-Pacific, Africa, and South America regions, as well as the Gulf-Cooperation Council (GCC) certification for the Middle East. Additionally, we have established semi-knocked-down (SKD) assembly and sales operations in more than 20 countries, such as Algeria and Kazakhstan.

 

SAGMOTO is a new brand initiative by Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group aimed at international market. SAGMOTO provides customized solutions to overseas customers, meeting their diverse needs and uncovering potential demands in local markets. In the future, we will continue to advance localized production and sales, enhance after-sales services, and innovate in financial support, thus establishing a novel framework for international market.

Quality Management

Putting customers' satisfaction in the first place, Shaanxi Automobile upholds the concept of producing superior products and providing excellent services. This ethos has led to the establishment of an intelligence and dual-cycle quality management model.

Dual cycle: Innovation-Driven Positive Development Cycle & Demand-Driven Continuous Improvement Cycle.

China 3C certification, ISO9001\TS16949 quality management system

Product Certification

By selecting leading certification bodies, updating real-time synchronisation of regulatory across markets,  interpreting accurately the regulatory requirements, ensuring professionalism and effectiveness of the product certification, we have gained access to the most countries around the world in the field of product certification. At present, SAG Commercial Vehicle Company has achieved UK Conformity Assessed (UKCA), the Technical Regulations of the Customs Union conformity Certificates (CU-TR), the Algerian vehicle access, the Gulf-Cooperation Council (GCC) certification for the Middle East, the Australia Design Rules (ADR), the Vietnam Register, the Vehicle Type Approval (VTA) for Malaysia, the Normas Oficiales Mexicanas (NOM) for Mexico, etc.
