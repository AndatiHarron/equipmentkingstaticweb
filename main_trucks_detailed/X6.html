SAGMOTO X6 Series

Agile and Lightweight, Yet Powerful.

X6 series of products have a wide range of total weights design and are suitable for various applications. With features of lightweight, low fuel consumption, and high cost-effectiveness, they are widely used in medium and long-distance logistics and urban special vehicles.

scroll down

Streamlined exterior design

• The combined headlamps are styled in keeping with the rigid body style, with turn signals, low beams, high beams and daytime running lights arranged in a neat and orderly sequence.

• The rear-view mirror applys a one-piece design, which is aesthetically pleasing and at the same time reduces wind resistance. Functionally integrated with electric heating and electric adjustment, there is also a close-proximity mirror + lower mirror, which provides a view of the lower right side of the vehicle, the lower front, to ensure the safety.

Interior design display

Full consideration of ergonomics for the layout of the cab. The instrument panel adopts the semi-enclosed structure design of the car, and all the buttons are within reach to ensure driving safety.

Human-machine interface interior design of the cab includes a multifunctional steering wheel, ensuring that all buttons are within easy reach.

 

The combination LCD instrument electronic screen covers a wide range of driving information, providing clear and intuitive display for easy reading.

 

The interior colour scheme is based on brown, black and grey. The centre console has an optional 10-inch multimedia screen with a piano lacquer panel and LCD display of multimedia, Bluetooth, AC and other control switches.

 

Automatic temperature control air conditioning system adjusts the airflow based on the temperature automatically, allowing the driver to drive with more concentration.

 

Large curved panoramic windshield provides a wide and unobstructed view, cooperating with the large curved rearview mirrors and compensating mirror to eliminate blind spots to the maximum extent.

 

Main airbag shock-absorbing seat guarantees the comfort of daily driving, and the multi-directional adjustment function of seat helps reduce driver fatigue.

 

The 800mm widened lower berth makes your driving more comfortable.

 

100L large storage space in the top cabinet and the 1200W high-power inverter power supply make your journey more convenient.

 

High load capacity; wide range of speed ratios

With light weight, low fuel consumption, driving comfort and high cost performance, it can easily cope with poor working conditions.

Modular lightweight design, using aluminum alloy and high-strength material to enhance the vehicle's effective load-bearing capacity.

The vehicle is equipped with a 400L large-capacity fuel tank, and an optional 600L fuel tank is available. With a full tank, it can travel over 2000km, meeting the requirements for long-distance transportation.

The short wheelbase design allows for a small turning radius of the vehicle. The STR wheel-side reduction axle can be selected, which can be equipped with a shortened axle to meet the regulations of narrow roads and provide stronger adaptability to various working conditions.

The power match is precise, with good adaptability to various working conditions and high reliability. The engine oil, oil filter, and diesel filter are designed for long maintenance intervals. The components of valves are reduced, resulting in low failure rate and high reliability.
