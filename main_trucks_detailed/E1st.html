SAGMOTO E1st

E1ST China Heavy Duty Truck

E1st is a brand-new super heavy truck launched by SAGMOTO after the insight and research on the future development direction of logistics and transportation industry and customer needs. With excellent performance, comfort and all-round safety guarantee, it brings new transportation experience and more values to users.

scroll down

Exterior

• The whole vehicle adopts a star-shaped air intake grille with penetrating chrome-plated decoration. The whole front face is scientific and fashionable, with strong visual impact, and smooth adjustment of top air deflector.

• Irregular black headlights on both sides are unique in shape, integrating high and low beam lights, fog lights, turn signals and driving assistance lights. At the same time, it is equipped with the automatic switching function of high and low beam light, which improves the safety of driving at night.

• Electric heating rearview mirror, the upper and lower lenses support independent adjustment.

• Multi-sound insulation and noise reduction technologies, such as double-channel door seal, multi-layer glue seal, seal strengthening and engine noise reduction, have achieved NVH of 50.6dB, and the noise of vehicles is better than that of the same level in the industry whether idling or driving.

Interior Decoration

E1st is a brand-new super heavy truck launched by SAGMOTO after the insight and research on the future development direction of logistics and transportation industry and customer needs. With excellent performance, comfort and all-round safety guarantee, it brings new transportation experience and more values to users.

The interior adopts the design concept of car-class intelligent seat, and the design of flat floor is 2.13 meters high, which provides ample space for drivers and passengers. 

 

12.3-inch full LCD instrument, suspended central control large screen, full of sense of science and technology, display information is very rich. The large-size touch screen integrates audio-visual entertainment and 360 images. 

 

Multifunctional steering wheel with ACC adaptive cruise button 

 

Grammer luxury airbag cushioned seats, with one-button inflation and deflation, heating and ventilation functions, can be adjusted from multiple angles, effectively reducing the fatigue of long-distance driving. 

 

Frequency conversion air conditioner+low temperature lithium battery, 6 hours continuous operation.

 

 Equipped with a water dispenser and refrigerators

 

Chassis

Parts suppliers are from imported manufacturers like Knohl and Bosch, which ensures the excellent performance of the whole vehicle in details, thus improving the quality of the whole vehicle.

Cummins 13-liter engine has superior power performance by applying technologies such as high detonation pressure, predictive cruise and thermal management.

Eaton's latest generation of integrated AMT gearbox has a transmission efficiency of 99.8%, and has twelve functions such as starting on a ramp and taxiing in neutral.

The front and rear axles are all made of Hande maintenance-free series products which have an oil change cycle of 5 years or 600 thousand kilometers, therefore reduces the cost of car maintenance for users.

The vehicle model can be equipped with 850L+380L double fuel tank, and the cruising range can reach more than 3000km, which provides a reliable guarantee for long-distance transportation.

The braking system with the highest system pressure in the industry is safer to drive with a braking distance of 25 meters.

Intelligent active safety technologies: LDWS (lane departure warning) +AEBS (anti-collision warning) +TPMSR (tire pressure monitoring system), ASR (driving acceleration slip regulation), ESC (electronic stability control system)… +cab conforming to the ECE certification of the European Union, which fully guarantees the safety of vehicles, drivers and passengers.
