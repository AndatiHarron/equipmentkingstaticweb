Comfortable cab configuration

Fully consider ergonomics for cab layout. The instrument panel is designed with a semi-enclosed structure of sedan, and all buttons are within reach to ensure driving safety.

The Series 9 cabin features a fixed front and mechanically suspended rear, effectively cushioning and providing a smooth ride even on rough and bumpy surfaces.

 

The Series 9 instrument panel employs a design that combines analog gauges with an LCD screen, allowing for intuitive and clear reading of vehicle driving data.

 

The Series 7 features fully enclosed airline seats that conform seamlessly to the back and waist, with optimized sponge density in the cushions for a suitable firmness and softness, providing a more comfortable ride.

 

The steering wheel of Series 7is equipped with power steering to make driving easier for you.

 

The 7 series  features a widened sleeper bed for enhanced comfort during temporary breaks.

 

The Series 6 product features an air-cushioned shock-absorbing seat for the driver, assuring comfort during daily driving; the seat can be adjusted in multiple directions to reduce driving fatigue.

 

The Series 6 cabin features a thickened floor with sound insulation and double-layered sealed doors, achieving industry-leading performance in noise reduction and dust prevention.

 

The width of the Series 6 cabin reaches 2240mm, allowing for more freedom and ease of movement.

 

Higher capacity, stronger off-road capabilities, and more diverse functions

Efficient power chain application and excellent chassis configuration bring you a more outstanding experience.

Equipped with a specialized suspension system and efficient 4*4 drive, it excels in the toughest terrains. The chassis and upper structure are integrated in design, providing various functional products such as fuel tankers, water tankers, dump trucks, and cargo trucks.

Double-layer frame design with locally triple-layer reinforcement for enhanced torsion resistance and high durability.

9 Series/7 Series full-time four-wheel drive, 6 Series part-time four-wheel drive.

Reinforced oil pan guard, iron bumper, turn signal and taillight guards, offering high protection and enhancing vehicle adaptability in harsh road conditions.

The 9-series has a turning diameter of only 11.1m, stronger overall passability, easier U-turns, and a low cargo bed height for time-saving and labor-saving loading and unloading.
